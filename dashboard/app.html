Statistical Experiments
Controlled A/B testing with hypothesis validation — each experiment uses Student's two-sample t-test at α = 0.05 significance level
📐
Methodology: Experiments follow a randomized controlled trial design. Users are randomly assigned to control/treatment groups. Statistical significance is evaluated using a two-sample Student's t-test with 95% confidence intervals. Only experiments with p < 0.05 are marked as significant.
